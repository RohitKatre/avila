Our Services

To cater to the requirements of International students, AVILA has business incorporations in UAE, EU, and Singapore. We provide numerous types of services stated below:

Visa services

Visa, like consular services for which a certain amount of fees are charged to grant visas in accordance with the scale laid down by the government. We provide visa services with great expertise and a considerable success rate, to cater to the requirements of students we offer the service of student visas as well.

Citizenship

We also help our clients ease the process of getting citizenship abroad. For a person who has to visit abroad on a frequent basis, visa services and even legal citizenship are a mindful option.

Residency Services

To help you achieve your dream of living abroad, we provide the service of residency to all Indians who want to reside in other countries. For Example UAE, Australia, etc.

Passport

To make your dream of studying and visiting places beyond your geographical areas, we help you achieve it by simplifying and easing the process of attaining your passport.

FRRO Services

FRRO services or foreign regional registration office services are specially designed for foreign nationals who long for staying in India for a significant period of time. The process of applying for FRRO services has been substantially simplified as even E-FRRO is available today.

Insurance services

Precaution is better than cure, for the same reason insurance services are the need of the hour with medical expenses skyrocketing, we thrive to cater to your needs and so smoothen the process of acquiring insurance services.
